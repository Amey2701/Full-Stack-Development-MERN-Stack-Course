Eye Makeup

The adage goes: The eyes are the windows to your soul. For centuries, men and women have been using eye makeup to enhance their looks. In ancient Egypt, people applied coal powder around their eyes to enhance their eyes and keep their skin from drying out in the desert. The earliest products in the market were simple coal liners, but today, there are a range of eye makeup products like casual eyeshadows, primers, under-eye concealers, mascaras, and a lot more that can bring out various looks. You can find a wide range of eye makeup online. You can even procure sophisticated products like a gold mascara, carnauba-based eyeliners, or vegan certified products with just the click of a button.

Importance

Eye makeup is used to draw attention to and enhance the eyes. It is mostly used by women, artists, stage performers, dancers, and actors. Makeup for the eyes is also a big part of the fashion and beauty industries. Eye cosmetics are essential to any makeup routine because it helps you create the look you prefer. For well-defined eyes, you need a lot of different kinds of makeup. The skin around our eyes is very thin and easily damaged.

A primer on eye makeup

A primer makes a layer between your skin and your makeup to protect it from any possible side effects. You can cover dark patches under your eyes with a concealer. The foundation cream makes your face smooth and provides the base for additional makeup. When putting on eyeshadow, you should start by putting a base colour on your eyelid to make your eye appear bigger. With eye shadow, glitter, and eyeliners, you can make different looks like shimmery eyes, cat eyes, smoky eyes, gradient eyes, and cut-crease eyes. But sometimes a kohl or eyeliner is all you need. As part of your eye cosmetics routine, you can also shape your eyebrows with a pencil or a brush. You can buy many eye makeup products from any online store and have them delivered to you.

What should be included in your eye makeup kit

Eyeliners: Eyeliners can make the eyelids stand out. The kajal is the first level or basic type of eye makeup product. Choose a liner that gives a jet-black colour in one stroke, like kohl. There are different eyeliners, such as pencil, liquid, and gel. You can also use liquid liners in the form of a pen, which are easier to use than the ones that come with a brush. Kohl that is made of gel or that doesn't run when it gets wet is a good choice because it stays put and doesn't smudge. Also, you can take it off easily without having to scrub them off.

Eye shadow: There are different kinds of eyeshadows, like liquid, powder, and cream. Select a product that is easy to use, lasts for a long time, and will not smudge. Products like powder eyeshadows blend easily and are great for people just starting out. Professionals use liquid eyeshadows and apply with a brush. Cream eyeshadows are thick and stay on for a long time. Note that the eyeshadow's shade or colour is important, so choose shades that match the colour of your eyes and skin.

Final touches: Eye makeup finishing comes in matte and glossy categories. The matte finish is not shiny and gives a more understated look, while glossy finish is shiny and is more noticeable. Choose the finish based on what you like. If you want a product to use every day, choose one with a matte finish. If you want something to wear to a party or outing, select the shiny look. Ensure that you include both in your eye makeup kit.

FAQs

What's the best way to use makeup to hide lines under the eyes?

You may use a daytime eye cream, which will blend better into your skin and make it seem prepped and lifted. The cream will also make it easier for your concealer to blend into your skin. If your under-eye area is excessively dry and wrinkles are too visible, you might try a cream or liquid concealer.

How can I find out if my eyes are makeup-sensitive?

Check if you are allergic to eye makeup. After applying makeup, if the skin around your eyes gets itchy, red, swollen, or scaly, you are probably having a reaction to one of your cosmetics. Additionally, the whites of your eyes may get red and puffy.

What eye makeup should a beginner use?

The first step in obtaining a perfect makeup look is to put together a beginner-friendly eye cosmetics kit. Your beginners eye makeup kit can include a mascara, eyeliner, foundation, primer, concealer, eyebrow gel and shadow palette. For a natural, everyday look, beginners should start with three to four basic shades. First, you need a highlighter that is one shade lighter than your skin tone, a mid-tone matte shade, and a contour shade that is two to three shades darker than your skin. Also, you will need brushes for eye makeup. For application, you'll need a small and mid-sized brush to help blend shades, a small flat eyeshadow brush, and a smudger, which is optional.
